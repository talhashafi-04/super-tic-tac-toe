Game Rules

How to Play Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe:

  • 9 smaller boards arranged in a 3√ó3 grid.
  • First player can place mark anywhere.
  • Last move position determines next player's board.
  • If sent to a won/full board, play on any open board.
  • Win by getting three boards in a row.
Pro tip: Place marks strategically to control opponent's next move!
